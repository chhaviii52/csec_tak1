COMPUTER SCIENCE ENGINEERS COMMUNITY

CSEC community at NIT Hamirpur is a vibrant student-led society focused on fostering a deep understanding of computer science and its diverse applications.We organise various coding competitions,technical workshops and hackathons to enhance your skills in programming and development.The community nurtures collaboration,innovation and peer learning.

Git/Github Workshop

An interactive session designed to intoduce participants about version control using Git.Perfect for beginners and intermediate developers.

Linux Workshop

An introduction to Linux operating system,focusing on its structure, essentail commands and utilities.It's an ideal session for developers.

Heuristics

A competitive event that onvolves solving problems and making decisions based on heuristics approach.These contests emphasize creativity ,experience and intuition.

Interviews

Focuses on a blend of technical skills, problem solving abilities and practical experience.However, interviews also access the ability to communicate and collaborate within a team
